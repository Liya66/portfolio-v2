Developer, educator, photograher, writer - Liya Wang
quite a while ago - infinity and beyond

I’ve listed some of my relevant experiences here. You might notice a few gaps — those are explained on my LinkedIn profile. You can read the facts and timelines, but still, you don’t know the whole story.

I was following a solid, traditional path — moving upward from education into a stable career. Then one day, I decided to move to Ireland to study. That’s what people see. But the truth is, I’ve always been passionate about technology — and that passion has quietly coexisted with everything else I’ve done.

I love using technology to automate processes, solve problems, and make things easier — no matter what field I’m in. After finishing my Arts degree, I explored different paths and eventually chose the most reliable one: becoming a teacher. But deep down, I always knew there was more to life than teaching English. I believed there were other ways to help people — to improve lives or even the world.

I actively experimented with this idea — from building digital linguistic corpora to adopting learning management systems for better educational workflows. Using technology to solve real problems fascinates me. Beyond the practical benefits like saving time or reducing errors, it gives me hope. It feels like we’re making progress — like we’re building toward something better.

I also take photos, both professionally and as a hobby. I like to freeze a moment in time — or even recreate it, depending on how I feel. Photography gives me the power to express my unique perspective and tell my own story. The feel of a fine machine in your hands is nice — but learning what it does and using it to create something meaningful? That’s magical. Feel free to check out more of my great photos on Instagram.
